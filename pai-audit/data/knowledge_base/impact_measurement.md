# Philanthropic Impact Measurement Framework

## QALY and DALY Metrics
QALY (Quality-Adjusted Life Year) measures years of life saved adjusted for quality of life. One QALY = one year in perfect health. DALY (Disability-Adjusted Life Year) measures years of healthy life lost due to disease or disability. One DALY averted = one year of healthy life saved. These metrics allow comparison across different health interventions.

## GiveWell Cost-Effectiveness Analysis
GiveWell uses a rigorous cost-effectiveness framework to evaluate charities. Key components:
1. **Direct Impact**: Measurable outcomes (lives saved, diseases prevented, income increased)
2. **Counterfactual**: What would have happened without the intervention
3. **Leverage**: Does the charity's work enable additional funding or action
4. **Robustness**: How sensitive are results to key assumptions

## Impact per Dollar Benchmarks
- **Highly Effective**: $50-$200 per DALY averted (GiveWell top charities)
- **Effective**: $200-$1,000 per DALY averted (e.g., some education programs)
- **Moderate**: $1,000-$5,000 per DALY averted (e.g., some infrastructure projects)
- **Low**: >$5,000 per DALY averted (many popular charities fall here)

## Rare Disease Philanthropy
Rare disease drug development costs $1-2 billion on average, but affects small patient populations. Philanthropic funding plays a critical role in early-stage research. Key metrics:
- Cost per patient enrolled in clinical trial: $50K-$200K
- Average time from discovery to approval: 10-15 years
- Success rate from Phase 1 to approval: ~8%
- Orphan Drug Act incentives: 50% tax credit on clinical trial costs, 7-year market exclusivity

## Measuring Non-Health Impact
For education and economic empowerment programs, alternative metrics include:
- **Years of Schooling Added**: Cost per additional year of education provided
- **Income Multiplier**: Increase in lifetime earnings per dollar invested
- **Social Return on Investment (SROI)**: Total social value created per dollar invested

## PAI ImpactLens Integration
PAI's ImpactLens module standardizes impact measurement across charities using QALY/DALY methodology. It provides:
1. Comparable impact scores across different cause areas
2. Cost-effectiveness rankings adjusted for evidence quality
3. Uncertainty ranges reflecting confidence in estimates
4. Radar chart visualization for multi-dimensional comparison
